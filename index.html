How To Use

close

On the map there are white dots where items are, and translators where gates are. The translators present at the start are the original (but actual) colors. E.g. the gate in Meeting Grounds is Violet even though it appears Amber.

Left Click

  • On a white item dot, marks it collected (green dot) or undoes this action.
  • On a translator, cycles through the 4 colors, starting at the original color - useful for randomized translators! This will also update any relevant item requirements :)
  • On an arrow, moves across different areas in the same region.
  • On the left menu, the area names will load the respective maps.
  • The transparent icons toggle between collected and not collected.

Middle Click

  • On an item, shows information about that item, including: the room name, its vanilla item, what the actual item is if marked, required items to collect it, and notes (if present).

Right Click

  • On an item dot (white or green), allows you to select an item to mark it on the map. You may then mark it collected (green dot) or undo the action (shows item icon again).
